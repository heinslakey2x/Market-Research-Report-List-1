<p><p>The plastic pellet market comprises various types, each serving distinct applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and resistant to moisture, ideal for packaging. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) is widely used for beverages due to its strength and recyclability. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers durability for products like containers. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile for pipes and construction materials. PP (Polypropylene) is known for its stiffness and heat resistance. ABS (Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene) is favored for its toughness in consumer products. Other plastics encompass a range of specialty materials for niche applications.</p></p>
