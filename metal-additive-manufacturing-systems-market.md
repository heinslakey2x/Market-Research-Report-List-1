<p><strong>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Bed Fusion</li><li>Directed Energy Deposition</li><li>Binder Jetting</li><li>Sheet Lamination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Metal Additive Manufacturing Systems market comprises several key technologies. Powder Bed Fusion uses a laser or electron beam to melt metal powder layers, creating intricate designs. Directed Energy Deposition involves melting material as it is deposited, allowing for repair and additive functionalities. Binder Jetting utilizes a binding agent to bond metal powders, which are later sintered for strength. Sheet Lamination involves stacking layers of metal sheets, which are then cut and fused, offering cost-effective manufacturing solutions. Each method serves distinct application needs and material properties.</p></p>
