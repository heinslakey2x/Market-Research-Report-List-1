<p><p>The GaN Power Module market can be categorized into two main types: Embedded Type and Ordinary Type. Embedded Type modules integrate Gallium Nitride (GaN) devices within a circuit board, optimizing space and enhancing thermal management for compact applications. In contrast, Ordinary Type modules are standalone units that require separate housing, making them versatile for various applications but often bulkier. Both types offer high efficiency, fast switching speeds, and are widely used in power converters, electric vehicles, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
