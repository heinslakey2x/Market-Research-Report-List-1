<p><p>The Industrial Process Chiller market primarily consists of two types: air-cooled chillers and water-cooled chillers. Air-cooled chillers utilize ambient air to dissipate heat, making them more suitable for locations with limited water access. They are generally easier to install and maintain. In contrast, water-cooled chillers rely on water to transfer heat, providing higher efficiency and cooling capacity, making them ideal for large-scale industrial applications. Each type serves distinct needs based on operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
