<p><p>Wireless communication technology for vehicles includes various market types, notably Dedicated Short-Range Communication (DSRC) and Mesh networks. DSRC allows vehicles to communicate directly with each other and infra-structure within short distances, enhancing safety and traffic efficiency. In contrast, Mesh networks enable devices to connect and relay information across a broader area, creating a self-healing network that improves communication reliability. Both technologies play crucial roles in enabling intelligent transportation systems and enhancing vehicle-to-everything (V2X) communication.</p></p>
