<p><p>The Hybrid Wireless Fire Detection System market includes various modes: </p><p>1. **Conventional Mode**: Utilizes a basic setup where detectors group together, triggering alarms collectively but lacking precise location identification. </p><p>2. **Addressable Mode**: Offers enhanced capabilities by enabling each device to communicate its status, allowing for more precise alarm identification and faster response times.</p><p>3. **Stand-alone Mode**: Functions independently without a centralized control system, ideal for smaller areas.</p><p>4. **Other Modes**: Encompasses specialized systems tailored for unique environments or requirements, integrating advanced technologies for fire detection.</p></p>
