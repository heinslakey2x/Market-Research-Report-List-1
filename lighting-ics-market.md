<p><strong>The Lighting ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AC-DC LED Driver IC</li><li>DC-DC LED Driver IC</li><li>LED Dimming Driver IC</li><li>Linear LED Driver IC</li><li>Ballast IC</li><li>NFC IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lighting ICs market encompasses various types, each tailored for specific applications. AC-DC LED driver ICs convert alternating current to direct current for LED lighting. DC-DC LED driver ICs regulate voltage and current for efficient LED operation. LED dimming driver ICs enable brightness control, enhancing energy efficiency. Linear LED driver ICs provide a simple solution for low-power applications. Ballast ICs manage fluorescent light systems, while NFC ICs facilitate communication in smart lighting solutions, improving connectivity and automation in lighting infrastructures.</p></p>
