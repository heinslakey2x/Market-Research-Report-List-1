<p><p>Parting waxes are essential in mold-making processes, classified into three main types. Conventional parting wax offers a simple release agent, easily applied but requiring frequent reapplication. Semi-permanent parting wax provides a longer-lasting solution, forming a durable barrier that enhances mold longevity. Silicone-free parting wax caters to specific applications where silicone residues could interfere with subsequent processes, ensuring compatibility with various materials. Each type serves distinct needs in the manufacturing and casting industries, balancing ease of use and performance.</p></p>
