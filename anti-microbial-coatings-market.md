<p><p>The antimicrobial coatings market is segmented into three main types: antimicrobial powder coatings, surface modifications, and coatings. Antimicrobial powder coatings are specialized formulations that provide protective surfaces resistant to microbial growth and contamination, typically used on various substrates. Surface modifications involve altering the physical or chemical properties of surfaces to inhibit microbial adhesion and proliferation. Lastly, coatings are liquid formulations applied to surfaces to create a barrier, preventing bacteria and fungi from thriving, thereby enhancing hygiene and longevity of products.</p></p>
