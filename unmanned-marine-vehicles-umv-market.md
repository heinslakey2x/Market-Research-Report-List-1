<p><p>Unmanned Marine Vehicles (UMVs) encompass three main market types: semi-autonomous, autonomous, and remote-controlled. Semi-autonomous UMVs can operate independently for certain tasks but require human oversight for complex decisions. Autonomous UMVs operate independently without human intervention, utilizing advanced algorithms for navigation and decision-making. Remote-controlled UMVs rely on direct human control via a communication link, allowing operators to maneuver them in real time. Each type serves distinct applications in industries like defense, research, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
