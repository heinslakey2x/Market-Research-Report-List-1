<p><p>The Energy Recovery Ventilator (ERV) market comprises various heat exchanger types. The plate and frame type heat exchanger employs parallel plates to transfer heat between air streams efficiently. Heat pipe heat exchangers utilize the phase change of a refrigerant for heat transfer, offering high efficiency in recovering energy. Rotary type heat exchangers feature a rotating wheel that alternates between exhaust and incoming air, allowing for effective thermal transfer. Other market options may include advanced technologies and hybrid systems that enhance energy recovery performance.</p></p>
