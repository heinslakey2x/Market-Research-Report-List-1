<p><p>Thermal spray coating machines utilize various methods to apply coatings for enhancing surface properties. Flame spray uses a fuel source to melt materials that are then sprayed onto a substrate. Electric arc wire spray employs an electric arc to melt the wire feedstock, effectively coating surfaces. Plasma spray generates a high-temperature plasma to deposit materials with superior adhesion and properties. Other methods may include cold spray and high-velocity oxygen fuel (HVOF) techniques, catering to diverse industrial applications and requirements.</p></p>
