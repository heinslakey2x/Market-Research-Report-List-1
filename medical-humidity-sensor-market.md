<p><strong>The Medical Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical humidity sensor market is categorized into relative and absolute humidity sensors. Relative humidity sensors measure the moisture content in the air as a percentage of the total moisture capacity at a given temperature, making them useful for applications requiring environmental control. Absolute humidity sensors, on the other hand, quantify the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, regardless of temperature, which is critical for precise monitoring in medical environments where accurate humidity levels are essential for patient care and equipment performance.</p></p>
