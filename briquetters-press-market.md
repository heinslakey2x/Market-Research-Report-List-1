<p><p>The briquetting press market features three main types: screw briquette presses, hydraulic briquette presses, and punching briquette presses. Screw briquette presses use a rotating screw to compress biomass into briquettes, providing a continuous process suitable for various raw materials. Hydraulic briquette presses apply high pressure using hydraulic systems, ideal for dense briquettes and a variety of materials. Punching briquette presses utilize a punching mechanism for shaping, offering flexibility in briquette size and density, making them versatile for different applications.</p></p>
