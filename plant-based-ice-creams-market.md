<p><p>The plant-based ice cream market can be categorized into two main types: single plant and blend plant. Single plant ice creams are made from one primary ingredient source, such as almond, coconut, or cashew, offering a distinct flavor profile. In contrast, blend plant ice creams combine multiple plant sources, such as nuts and legumes, to achieve a creamier texture and more complex taste. Both market types cater to growing consumer demand for dairy-free alternatives and align with health-conscious and environmentally friendly trends.</p></p>
