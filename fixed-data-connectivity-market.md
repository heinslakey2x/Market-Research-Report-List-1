<p><p>Fixed Data Connectivity Market consists of two primary types: Wireless Type and Wired Type. </p><p>Wireless Type relies on radio signals, satellite, or microwave technologies to provide internet access, offering greater flexibility and mobility for users. It is typically used in remote or difficult-to-wire locations.</p><p>Wired Type, on the other hand, utilizes physical cables, such as fiber optics or copper, to deliver high-speed internet. It is known for its stability, higher bandwidth, and lower latency, making it ideal for urban and densely populated areas.</p></p>
