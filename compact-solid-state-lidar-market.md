<p><p>The compact solid-state LiDAR market features three primary types: </p><p>1. **MEMS-Based Scanning**: Utilizes Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems for precise angular scanning, making it ideal for applications requiring detailed 3D mapping.</p><p>2. **Phased Array**: Employs multiple small antennas to electronically steer the laser beam, enabling rapid scanning without moving parts, offering high-resolution data.</p><p>3. **Non-Scanning Flash**: Captures a complete scene in a single flash of laser light, providing swift data acquisition, which is particularly useful for applications like autonomous driving and robotics.</p></p>
