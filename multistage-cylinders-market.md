<p><p>The multistage cylinders market includes two primary types: single-acting and double-acting cylinders. Single-acting cylinders operate in one direction, utilizing spring force or external pressure to retract, making them suitable for simple applications where only one stroke is needed. In contrast, double-acting cylinders allow fluid pressure to be applied in both directions, enabling greater power and control for tasks requiring movement in both directions. These cylinders are widely used in mobile equipment, manufacturing, and automation systems.</p></p>
