<p><p>The online dermatology consultation market is divided into two main types: asynchronous and synchronous consultations. Asynchronous consultations allow patients to submit their skin concerns and images for evaluation at their convenience, with dermatologists responding later. This model provides flexibility and is suited for non-urgent issues. In contrast, synchronous consultations involve real-time interactions, typically through video calls, enabling immediate diagnosis and treatment recommendations. This approach is more suitable for urgent cases where instant communication is essential for patient care.</p></p>
