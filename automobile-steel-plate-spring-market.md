<p><p>The automobile steel plate spring market includes two primary types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of multiple layers of steel leaves, providing enhanced load-bearing capacity and flexibility, making them suitable for heavy-duty vehicles. In contrast, mono-leaf springs, commonly known as leaf springs, feature a single steel plate that offers a more compact design, ideal for lighter vehicles. Both types are crucial in vehicle suspension systems, contributing to ride comfort and stability.</p></p>
