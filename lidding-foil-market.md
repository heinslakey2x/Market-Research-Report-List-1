<p><p>Lidding foil market is categorized into three main types. Single Layer (Push Through) foil consists of a simple, single-layer structure that allows easy access to the product. Two-Layer (Paper/Foil) combines paper and foil, offering enhanced barrier properties and puncture resistance, ideal for perishable goods. Child Resistant Foil features complex designs that prevent children from opening packages, ensuring safety while maintaining product integrity. Each type serves distinct packaging needs, balancing accessibility, protection, and security.</p></p>
