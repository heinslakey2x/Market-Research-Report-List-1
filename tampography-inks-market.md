<p><ul><li>Single Component Type</li><li>Two Component Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tampography inks are categorized into single component and two component types based on their formulation and application. Single component inks are ready-to-use formulations that do not require mixing and are typically used for simpler applications. In contrast, two component inks consist of a base and a hardener, which must be mixed before use. This type often provides superior adhesion and durability, making it suitable for more demanding surfaces and conditions, enhancing the ink's performance in tampographic printing processes.</p></p>
