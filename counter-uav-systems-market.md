<p><p>Counter-UAV systems can be categorized into three main types: ground-based, air-based, and handheld systems. Ground-based systems typically consist of stationary or mobile installations designed to detect and neutralize drones from the ground, often utilizing radar and jamming technology. Air-based systems include UAVs equipped with countermeasures to intercept and disable malicious drones mid-flight. Handheld systems offer portability, allowing users to deploy counter-UAV measures easily in various environments, often incorporating signal disruption or net capture technologies for immediate response.</p></p>
