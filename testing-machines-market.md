<p><p>The testing machines market primarily includes two types: electromechanical and hydraulic testing machines. Electromechanical testing machines utilize electric motors to apply loads and measure material properties with high precision, making them suitable for various applications like tensile and compression tests. In contrast, hydraulic testing machines use fluid power to generate force, allowing for higher load capacity and versatility in testing larger samples. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and materials research, ensuring safety and quality standards.</p></p>
