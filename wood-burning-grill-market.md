<p><p>The wood burning grill market primarily consists of charcoal and wood pellet types. In contrast, wood pellet grills utilize compressed wood pellets as fuel, offering convenience and precise temperature control, along with a variety of wood flavors. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with charcoal appealing to those who favor classic grilling techniques, while wood pellets attract users seeking versatility and ease of use.</p></p>
