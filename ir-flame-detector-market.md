<p><p>The IR flame detector market comprises three main types: Single IR, Dual IR, and Multi IR detectors. Single IR detectors sense a specific wavelength, suitable for environments with limited interference. Dual IR detectors combine two wavelengths, enhancing sensitivity and reducing false alarms in challenging conditions. Multi IR detectors utilize multiple wavelengths, allowing for broader detection capabilities and improved discrimination against ambient light and other heat sources. Each type serves distinct applications, balancing sensitivity, reliability, and response time.</p></p>
