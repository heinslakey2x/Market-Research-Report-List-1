The ECMO market is divided into three main types: Veno-Arterial (VA) ECMO, which supports both heart and lung function; Arterio-Venous (AV) ECMO, primarily used for patients with severe respiratory issues; and Veno-Venous (VV) ECMO, which focuses mainly on oxygenating blood for patients with respiratory failure without affecting cardiac function.</p></p>
