<p><p>The specimen retrieval bag market is categorized into two main types: detachable and non-detachable bags. Detachable bags feature a design that allows them to be separated from the retrieval device after use, facilitating easier specimen handling and disposal. In contrast, non-detachable bags remain permanently attached to the retrieval instrument, providing a secure containment method during the entire procedure. Each type serves specific surgical needs, enhancing efficiency and safety in procedures involving the collection of biological specimens.</p></p>
