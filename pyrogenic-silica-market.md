<p><p>Pyrogenic silica, also known as fumed silica, is a highly versatile, lightweight, and fine powder produced by the combustion of silicon tetrachloride in the presence of oxygen. It is widely used as a reinforcing agent in various applications, including coatings, adhesives, sealants, and personal care products due to its exceptional properties like improved viscosity, thixotropy, and anti-caking abilities.</p><p>The pyrogenic silica market is on an upward trajectory, driven by growing demand in industries such as automotive, electronics, and cosmetics. The increasing focus on lightweight materials and the advancement of technology in manufacturing processes are propelling market growth. Moreover, the rising use of pyrogenic silica in the production of high-performance tires and its adoption in food and pharmaceuticals for anti-caking and flow improvement are further enhancing its market presence.</p><p>The Pyrogenic Silica Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period.
