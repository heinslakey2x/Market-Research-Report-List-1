<p><ul><li>Linear AC Power Sources</li><li>PWM AC Power Sources</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC power source market is primarily divided into two types: linear AC power sources and PWM (Pulse Width Modulation) AC power sources. Linear AC power sources provide stable and low-distortion output voltage, making them ideal for sensitive electronic testing. In contrast, PWM AC power sources use rapid switching to generate AC signals, allowing for adjustable frequency and amplitude. PWM sources are often favored for their efficiency and compact design, catering to a broader range of applications, including renewable energy systems and industrial automation.</p></p>
