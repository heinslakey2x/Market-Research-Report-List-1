<p><p>The technical enzymes market is segmented into various types, each serving specific industrial applications. Amylases break down starches into sugars, facilitating processes in food and beverage production. Cellulases degrade cellulose, enhancing fiber processing and biofuel production. Proteases are crucial for protein breakdown, commonly used in detergents and food industries. Lipases catalyze the breakdown of fats and oils, important in food, cosmetics, and biofuel sectors. Other enzymes include oxidases and transferases, catering to diverse industrial needs and innovations.</p></p>
