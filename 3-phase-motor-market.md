<p><ul><li>Synchronous Motors</li><li>Induction Motors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-phase motor market primarily comprises synchronous and induction motors. Synchronous motors operate at constant speed, maintaining synchronization with the power supply frequency, making them ideal for high-efficiency applications. Induction motors, more commonly used, rely on electromagnetic induction and are known for their simplicity and low cost. Both types are essential in industrial applications, with induction motors dominating due to their robustness and ease of maintenance, while synchronous motors are favored in applications requiring precise speed control.</p></p>
