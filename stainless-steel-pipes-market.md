<p><ul><li>Welded</li><li>Seamless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel pipes market is primarily divided into two types: welded and seamless pipes. Welded pipes are made by joining two or more pieces of stainless steel, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility for various applications. They are commonly used in structural projects and fluid transport. Seamless pipes, on the other hand, are crafted from a solid round steel billet, resulting in stronger and more reliable pipes. Seamless pipes are preferred for high-pressure applications, such as oil and gas industries, due to their integrity and resistance to corrosion.</p></p>
