<p><p>The HVAC valves market includes various types such as ball valves, globe valves, butterfly valves, and others. Ball valves are known for their quick shut-off ability and low flow resistance, making them ideal for on/off applications. Globe valves are preferred for throttling flow due to their design, promoting precise flow control. Butterfly valves are lightweight and efficient for regulating large volumes of fluid with minimal pressure drop. Other types include check valves and solenoid valves, catering to specific HVAC system requirements.</p></p>
