<p><p>CW Fiber Amplifiers are categorized into two main types based on their operational characteristics: broadband and single frequency. Broadband amplifiers provide wide spectral coverage, making them suitable for various applications requiring multiple wavelengths, such as complex communication systems. In contrast, single frequency amplifiers focus on a specific wavelength, offering high output power and efficiency for applications needing precise signal amplification, like in some laser systems. Both types enhance signal strength in optical networks, catering to distinct technological needs.</p></p>
